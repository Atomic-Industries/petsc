To celebrate the twentieth anniversary of the release of PETSc 2.0, we will host a workshop on the past and future of PETSc at the Argonne Theory and Computing Sciences Conference Center.
  • Presentations on large-scale application codes that utilize PETSc
  • Presentations by the developers of widely used simulation packages that use PETSc, including libMesh, MOOSE, SLEPc, TAO, and PETSc4py
  • Discussion of future directions of PETSc, including
    • extreme-scale architectures
    • multicore systems and gpus
    • multiphysics, multiscale support
    • ODE integrators
    • uncertainty quantification, stochastic PDEs,
    • web-friendly features
  • Presentations on complementary software, such as FEniCS and Trilinos
Send mail to petsc-20@mcs.anl.gov and we will contact you as soon as registration is open, also let us know at petsc-20@mcs.anl.gov if you need financial support to attend.
