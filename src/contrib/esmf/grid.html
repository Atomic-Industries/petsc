1 DGrid

1.1 Description

DGrid (distributed or decomposed grid) contains a decomposition over nodes and threads as well as a grid specification.

1.2 Requirements

(very incomplete)

A grid may be defined on a specific set of nodes or threads, and different grids within the same executable may be mapped to different sets of
nodes and threads.  The way grids are mapped may change over the course of model execution.  Global grids and subsets (masks, regional grids) must be supported.  Time dependent grid subsets, such as satellite swaths, are a projected capability.

Horizontal grids
    Gaussian grid/Reduced grid
    Spectral grid, general pentagonal truncation
    Displaced pole grid
    Arakawa B, C, D grids in spherical coordinates
    Icosahedral grid
    Cubed sphere grid

    Some references below.
