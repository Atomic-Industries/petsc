The PETSc Users Mailing List

The PETSc users mailing list is intended for communication among users of the PETSc software. Do NOT send bug reports to this list, but instead to petsc-maint.mcs.anl.gov.

You can subscribe to the PETSc users mailing list by sending electronic mail to majordomo@mcs.anl.gov containing in its body the two words subscribe petsc-users. You can unsubscribe by sending a message to the same address conatining the two words subscribe petsc-users.
